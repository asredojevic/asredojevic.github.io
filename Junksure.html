Junksure

ux/ui and visual design

Design an experience that makes it easier for sellers to intelligently inventory their goods, and helps bargain hunters find the needle in a haystack.The seller path is standardized with list and category views, compared to the browser path that is fluid and visually dynamic. Since images are a huge feature with any product, I decided that they should visually represent the difference between the two users. In the seller interface, all the images are the same size to easily organize and find items, while in the browser interface, the images are all different sizes for chance discoveries with every scroll.
