PROBLEM
From a survey sent out quarterly, we received feedback that users were experiencing difficulty searching over 3 million parts and found that our search algorithm only returned valuable results when users entered a specific part number.
After looking at what our users were entering into the search bar, we discovered that around 58% of arrow.com searches were either family names, part numbers or part names. Meaning we were only capturing users at the end of their sourcing journey instead of the beginning.
